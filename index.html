Video Marketing Workshop

What You'll Learn

Make Videos That Don't Get Skipped

Learn how to shoot, edit, and produce scroll-stopping marketing videos in this hands-on workshop. You'll work with a real business and create content they can actually use—while learning the same video techniques used by marketers and influencers. This workshop is for students interested in video production or promotion. No experience required.

In this hands-on video marketing workshop, you'll learn how to:

Shoot professional-looking video
Use lighting that makes your content pop
Capture clean audio like a pro
Edit videos that grab attention

Then you'll produce a real promotional video for a local business—something you can actually add to your portfolio.
